Fallen Angels is an Odin based FFXIV Free Company. We aim to maintain a helpful, mature and no-pressure environment to house old and new players alike. We have a discord server and hold regular community events for fun and prizes. Our mansion is at Plot 5, Ward 1 (Mist). Please feel free to take a look! If you are interested in joining, send a tell (or mail) to Noms Noms or Mie Mie in-game.
